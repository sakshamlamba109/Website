Registering using QR Code


This technology addresses the whole process of registering a newly purchased product against warranty and synchronizing the records of CLIENT, SELLER, and MANUFACTURER with 0% involvement of physical records and paperwork (Warranty cards, invoices, warranty forms, quickstart guide …). As earlier, it was a tedious task as the whole process required a lot of paperwork, this technology if implemented successfully would simply register any client with the manufacturer by just scanning the QR code or the barcode. 
The proposed solution was presented for the Tata Crucible National Hackathon and my team was selected for the North Region Finals. The work is under progress and the Hackathon finals are in month of May. The architectural diagram is given below.
